Migration to Shopify

Our wide eCommerce expertise allows us to say that it is a platform worth consideration. Transferring a store to another platform can be a tough ordeal. We’ll make your Shopify migration safe, easy and quick, making sure that nothing is lost along the way. On the new website you get:

  • Same rankings
  • All products with their descriptions, images, reviews, metadata, etc
  • All pages from the previous website
  • Any functionality from the previous website that we can adapt and implement
  • No-bugs guarantee and 1 month of support free of charge.

Web Accessibility

Around 18% of internet users are people with disabilities. If your shop is accessible, it means that all people can use your services and place an order, whether they suffer from permanent or temporary disabilities or not, so accessible website improves your website’s SEO and increases your market share.

Making your website accessible means helping all people to use all the services available on it.

By following Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) we will make your website easy to use for everyone and, therefore, increase your sales and brand loyalty.

We can help you to build or make existing store accessible for everyone!
